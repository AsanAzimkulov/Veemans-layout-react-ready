What is Veemans ?

Veemans is a game where you get tokens for moving with a transport. It's simple.

Win in competitions versus other players, upgrade your car and capture territories to get more tokens and rewards.

What is DRIVE 2 EARN?

It is a new concept that suggests the possibility of virtual earnings for the actions a user performs in the real world.

To enroll, you need to link your account with a mobile device. Each user earns tokens for daily movements by any type of transport with a speed from 10 to 60 km/h.

Elements of the Veemans Ecosystem

  1. Alliances

    Create your own alliances in your city, capture the territory and charge other players for traveling through.

  2. Car Dealers

    Become a car dealer and sell NFT cars to other users on our marketplace.

  3. Augmented reality

    Enjoy your amazing car through your phone.

  4. Garage

    Upgrade your veeman in your personal garage to raise more tokens from traveling.

  5. Quests

    Find tasks on the map and complete quests in augmented reality.

  6. Car Sharing

    You do not have your own car or you do not travel much? No problem, just buy NFT Veeman, rent it out and earn income.

  7. PvP - Competitions

    Take part in tournament races and get prizes and bonuses.

  8. Marketplace

    The marketplace and wallet built into the application allows you to buy and sell NFTs.
